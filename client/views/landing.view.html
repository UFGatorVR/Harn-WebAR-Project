Welcome to an Augmented Reality (AR) Art Experience

    Augmented Reality (AR) refers to any computer generated images or animations superimposed onto the real world. Students from the University of Florida’s Gator Virtual Reality Club collaborated with the museum to produce 3 unique AR installations for 3 different works of art.

    Adjacent to Yayoi Kusama’s Nets-Infinity (TWOS), Jeff Joyce’s Veil, and Michael Light’s Full Moon (Edward White Spacewalking Above Texas: Photo by James McDi) are special wall markers. Each marker has its own 3D AR content. To view the content:

    1. Tap the “Enter!” button on the bottom of your screen.
    2. Hold up your smartphone so the marker image is within the black square brackets.
    3. Press the triangle “Play” button
    4. Experience Augmented Reality (AR) inspired by the art.

    To view these instructions again, press the “Back” button in the upper left-hand corner. If you have any questions, please visit the Bishop Study Center.
